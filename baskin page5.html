About Baskin Robbins

Baskin Robbins has been serving 31 flavors of ice cream since 1945. Our mission is to spread happiness through premium handcrafted ice creams, sundaes, desserts, and cakes. We believe in quality, creativity, and unforgettable taste.

Our Story

Founded by Burt Baskin & Irv Robbins, what started as a single ice cream shop has now become one of the largest dessert chains in the world. With innovation and passion, we continue introducing new flavors and exciting seasonal creations every year.

Our Values

  • High-quality ingredients
  • Customer satisfaction above all
  • Flavor innovation and creativity
  • Responsibility and community care
